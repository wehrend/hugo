    Pocket Operator Modular 400 in eurorack

    So I bought a low cost eurorack case from doepfer and a set of frontpanels plus power adapter for the pocket operator modular 400 from Oddvolt to put the said machine into a eurorack modular system. I also bought a small, but decent sequencer, since the one from teenage engineering is not very handy to use. I have two further modules on my list, the Doepfer A-119, an input module, as well as the Doepfer A-124, a wasp filter, to complete the setup.
